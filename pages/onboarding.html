⚡ One-time setup required for Ghost Sessions Required

Browsers block extensions from opening incognito windows by default — you need to allow it once. This takes about 20 seconds and never needs to be done again.

1
Open your browser's extensions page. Type this in your address bar:
🔵 Edge: edge://extensions
🦁 Brave: brave://extensions
🟡 Chrome: chrome://extensions
2
Find Ghost Tab in the list and click Details.
3
Scroll down and toggle "Allow in InPrivate" (Edge) or "Allow in Incognito" (Brave/Chrome) to ON.
4
Done! Come back and click the 👻 icon — Ghost Sessions will now work.

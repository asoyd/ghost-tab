## Full description

**Ghost Tab gives you two tools in one:**

---

### 👻 Ghost Sessions — true multi-account browsing

Open any URL in a fully isolated incognito window. Each Ghost Session has its own separate cookie jar — you can be logged in as User A in one window and User B in another, on the same site, at the same time.

**Save named sessions with multiple URLs.** One click opens them all together in a fresh incognito window. Perfect for:
- Developers testing with multiple user accounts
- Social media managers handling multiple profiles
- QA testers switching between environments
- Anyone who needs to be two places at once

> Login state is not saved between sessions (incognito by design). Pair with Bitwarden — enable it in incognito too and it autofills your credentials automatically.

---

### 🗂 Workspaces — one-click tab groups

Save groups of URLs that open all at once in a normal window. Cookies and logins persist between restarts. Think of it as a smarter bookmark folder.

- Save "Morning routine", "Client A", "Project X" — whatever makes sense for you
- Snapshot your current tabs with one click
- Open a full set of tabs in a new window instantly

### Permissions explained

- **tabs** — reads the current tab's URL when you click "Open current page"
- **contextMenus** — adds "Open in Ghost Tab" to the right-click menu
- **storage** — saves your sessions and workspaces locally on your device

No permission is used to read page content or track your browsing.

---

### Setup note for Ghost Sessions

Browsers block extensions from opening incognito windows by default. After installing, go to the extension's Details page and toggle "Allow in InPrivate" (Edge) or "Allow in Incognito" (Brave/Chrome) to ON. The onboarding screen walks you through it in under a minute.
